LA's Home Kitchens

This directory maps active MEHKO (Micro-Enterprise Home Kitchen Operation) licenses in Los Angeles.

Note: A license reflects a permit to operate, but it does not guarantee a kitchen is currently open. Many businesses may still be in the setup phase. Cuisine types and descriptions are automatically generated from permit and open data. These are intended as a guide and may contain errors. As these are private home kitchens, hours and contact info can change without notice.
